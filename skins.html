How Much Are Skins in Fortnite?

Fortnite exclusively uses an in-game currency called "V-BUCKS". The price for each item in the item shop depends on the rarity of the the item being purchased. This genral price point goes for all different in game cosmetics such as: skins, backpacks, gliders, pickaxes, emotes, gun wraps and trails. The only exception is when a special event skin gets added to the Fortnite item shop. The item shop gets reset every 24 hours. There are new skin released as well as old skins that come back. Because some skins have not been in the shop for a few years they become very rare and valuble.
